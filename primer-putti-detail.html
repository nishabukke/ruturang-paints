Primer

Ruturang Wall Primer Solvent Thinnable is the ideal foundation for masonry topcoats like distemper, emulsions, lustre, and matte wall finishes. It creates a smooth surface for putty and paint, ensuring a superior finish. Fast-drying and equipped with excellent healing properties, it also resists wall alkalinity, moisture, and fungus, making it the ultimate choice for wall preparation.

Features :
Opacity and Whiteness
Alkali Resistance
Good Sealing Properties
Fortified with Fungicides

Available Sizes : 1L, 4L, 10L, 20L

Protect Primer

Elevate your exterior painting projects with Ruturang Protect Wall Primer, designed for outstanding adhesion with all top coats. This water-based primer establishes a resilient film, significantly extending the lifespan of your complete paint system. Its resistance to chalking ensures suitability for application on recently cured plaster, providing enhanced brightness and exceptional coverage to rejuvenate your walls with a pristine, long-lasting finish.

Features :
Opacity and Whiteness
Enhances Top Coat Finish
Excellent Adhesion
Anti-Chalking

Available Sizes : 1Kg, 40Kg

Wall Putty

RUTURANG Wall Putty is a specialized white cement-based product that incorporates re-dispersible polymers and functional additives. This unique formulation is designed for the purpose of creating a sleek and refined surface on a variety of interior and exterior rough plaster substrates, ensuring a smooth and flawless finish.

Product Features :
  • 1. Renders a smooth and uniform finish on rough plaster surfaces.
  • 2. Offers commendable workability for ease of application.
  • 3. Requires no water-based curing process
  • 4. Exhibits notable resistance to water.
  • 5. Boasts strong tensile adhesion, compressive strength, and flexural strength.
  • 6. Regulates water vapour permeability effectively.
  • 7. Demonstrates exceptional resistance to abrasion.
  • 8. Inhibits the growth of algae and fungi on walls.
  • 9. Ensures robust intercoat adhesion with emulsion paints.
