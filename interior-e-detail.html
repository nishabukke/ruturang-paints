Prime Interior Emulsion

Ruturangs Prime Interior Emulsion provides an excellent option for transitioning from distempers to a more advanced paint solution. It delivers a finish comparable to plastic paint, all while maintaining a cost that is closely aligned with distemper. With its exceptional coverage, it offers higher coverage than of distemper, reducing the amount of material required. Furthermore, this emulsion lasts for a minimum of 3 to 4 years, extending the time between painting projects. Ruturangs Prime Emulsion ensures a smooth finish for your walls, making it an excellent choice for a cost-effective paint solution.

Features :
Higher Coverage
Low Maintenance
Affordability
Long Lasting
