Cool Star

Ruturang Cool Star, an advanced infrared heat-reflective paint engineered to combat heat and offer a cooling solution for interior spaces within buildings. Challenging the prevailing notion that coolness is exclusively associated with white exteriors, we recognize the paramount importance of vibrant and rich colors in the design of buildings for architects, builders, and homeowners. However, these vivid hues tend to absorb the sun's radiance, causing a gradual temperature increase throughout the day. This upsurge in heat not only leads to higher indoor temperatures but also amplifies energy consumption, thereby imposing a significant strain on utility bills, the environment, and global resources.

Our unwavering commitment to research and development in the realm of heat-reflective coatings has enabled Ruturang to harness an innovative color technology that operates on a dual-faceted principle:

  • 1. The initial bottom coat is infused with a low thermal conductivity paint, effectively limiting heat transfer from top to bottom.
  • 2. The top coat, serving as the second layer, is designed to reflect heat through selective infrared light reflection. This cutting-edge technology empowers us to offer coatings for various applications, such as concrete, metal sheet roofing, and cement sheet roofing, boasting an impressive solar energy reflection rate of 80% or more. This not only benefits our environment but also provides tangible advantages to consumers.

By applying both coats, each at a thickness of 150 microns, we can effectively reduce room temperatures by 5-6 degrees Celsius in high-temperature zones and by an impressive 9-10 degrees Celsius during direct sunlight exposure.
