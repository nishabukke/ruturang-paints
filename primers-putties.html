Primers & Putties

We understand that the foundation of any great paint job lies in the quality of primers and putties. That's why we're proud to offer a selection that goes above and beyond the ordinary. Our primers are engineered to provide the perfect canvas for your painting projects. From our Ultimate Adhesion Primer that ensures your paint adheres flawlessly to various surfaces, to our Rust-Inhibiting Primer, designed to safeguard metal against corrosion, we have a primer for every need. Our All-in-One Primer and Sealer simplifies your projects, saving you time and money.

When it comes to putties, our Super-Fine Surface Putty is your secret weapon for achieving a flawless finish. It easily fills and smoothes imperfections, dries quickly, and can be painted over seamlessly.
