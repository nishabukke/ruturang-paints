Damp Guard

Ruturang DampGuard offers a robust solution to address all your concerns related to leaks, cracks, and water seepage. These issues can crop up in spaces such as bedrooms, bathrooms, and kitchens, often as a result of weather conditions or humidity. Ruturang DampGuard provides a comprehensive waterproofing solution crafted with cutting-edge technology, ensuring enduring and effective results.

Ruturang DampGuard is versatile and suitable for application on a wide range of surfaces, including cementitious and masonry surfaces, pre-existing brick batcoba, over existing acrylic coatings, and cementitious boards on terraces. It also serves as an effective waterproofing basecoat for vertical walls.

Crack Bridging
